Our Story

Our story begins with a group of IT professionals trying out how to bring joy to people in the midst of pandemic. Noticing that many people love to eat unhealthy snacks, and considering the fact that its is more crucial nowadays to look after our health, the team came up with the idea of providing healthy snacks that would satisfy the cravings of the people while also increasing their nutrients intake. fter thinking about it for days, the team decide to create NutriBites, an M-commerce application offering delicious baked goods with a healthy twist!
